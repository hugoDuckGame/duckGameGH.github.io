Equitable - Check your products for nestle

Context : Nestle is a swiss company who owns almost half of existing food/cosmetics brands, but it has had it's lot of controversies, because
they dont respect human rights and steal resources from developping countries for exapmle by charging them for their own water. So, a lot of people including me have decided to boycott as they can the horrible brand. But as they own a lot of brands, it can be hard to know what products are from them and which are not so that's why i developped this tool where you can type in the barcode of a product and it will tell you if you can buy it or not.

Please insert EAN13 barcode value of the product to check if it comes from nestle
